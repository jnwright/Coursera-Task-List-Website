Battle Sephiroth
Player 1
Go to Radiant Garden (previously Hollow Bastion) and land on the Crystal Fissure. From there, head to where you completed the 1,000 Heartless Challenge at Dark Depths. Sephiroth should be waiting to fight. It's recommended that you are at least at level 40 and also have these items/abilities:
  • At least 6 elixirs
  • High Jump lvl. 2
  • Aerial Dodge lvl. 2
  • Guard
  • Counterguard
  • Retaliating Slash
  • Oblivion keyblade
